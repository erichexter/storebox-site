Blitz Mode

30 boxes in 60 minutes

Designed for the "I just moved and nothing is labeled" problem.

  1. Open Blitz Mode (lightning button), select your storage location
  2. Tap Snap Photo — point at the open box
  3. Analyze Photo — AI returns an item checklist instantly
  4. Uncheck anything wrong, tap Confirm and Next
  5. Next container auto-created. Repeat until done.
  6. Session summary: containers cataloged + total items added
